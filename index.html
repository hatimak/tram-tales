The event Tram Tales is a mixed media location-based cultural extravaganza celebrating the history of trams, while looking to its wonderful and exciting future as a symbol of the city of Calcutta. Using films/videos, music performances, live theatre, adda sessions, photography competitions, workshops, games and painting - we plan to bring the depot to life in a unique way that has never before done in India.

The Calcutta Tramways Company (1978) LTD has given us permission to use the beautiful Gariahat Tram Depot as our location for this event to be held on the 7th and 8th of May 2016 from 5pm to 8pm.

This is our humble tribute to the glorious trams of the city.
